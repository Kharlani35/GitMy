Oleksandr Usyk is a Honored Master of Sports of Ukraine, one of the most famous and decorated Ukrainian boxers of modern times.
In 2023, Usyk continued to hold his heavyweight championship titles, winning against Daniel Dubois on August 26, defending his WBO, WBA, IBF, and IBO titles.
This was another success that confirmed his status as an elite boxer.
The absolute world heavyweight champion Oleksandr Usyk (24-0, 15 KOs) defeated former IBF title holder Daniel Dubois (22-3, 21 KOs).
Usyk maintains an undefeated streak in the professional ring with 25 victories, 15 of them by knockout. He remains one of the best boxers of today and serves as an example of resilience and courage both in and out of the ring, actively supporting Ukraine during the war.
In addition to his boxing achievements, Oleksandr Usyk is the founder of his own clothing brand, USYK-17. The brand reflects his style, charisma, and national pride, offering high-quality and stylish clothing for true sports and Ukrainian culture enthusiasts.
The USYK-17 collections are distinguished by:

  • comfort
  • premium materials
  • unique designs
that inspire victories both in the ring and in life.
